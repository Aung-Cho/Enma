About

Enma is the japanese katana.Crafted by none other than the legendary swordsmith Shimotsuki Kozaburou, Enma is one of the 21 O Wazamono grade swords to exist in the world of One Piece. While Enma is currently being wielded by Roronoa Zoro, he wasn't the first user of this powerful blade. For years, this blade was in the usage by the strongest Samurai of Wano Country, Kozuki Oden.
